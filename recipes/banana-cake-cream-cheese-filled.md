Boxed banana bread layered with a sweet cream cheese filling and baked in an 8x8 pan.

## Ingredients
- 1 box Pillsbury banana bread mix
- 1 large egg (filling)
- 4 oz cream cheese (filling)
- 1/4 cup granulated sugar (filling)
- 3 tbsp flour (filling)

## Steps
1. **Batter:** Mix the banana muffin recipe from the box.
2. **Filling:** Combine the egg, cream cheese, granulated sugar, and flour to make the cream cheese filling.
3. **Assemble & Bake:** Spray an 8 x 8 pan. Layer half the batter, then the cream cheese filling, then the remaining batter. Bake at 325-350.

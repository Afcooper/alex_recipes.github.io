A thicker version of Chinese brown sauce built on beef broth, oyster sauce, and minced garlic.

## Ingredients
- 1 cup beef broth
- 1 1/4 tbsp oyster sauce
- 1 tsp soy sauce (low sodium)
- 3/4 tsp sugar
- 2 tbsp cornstarch (corn flour or potato starch)
- 1 clove garlic (minced)

## Steps
1. Add broth, oyster sauce, soy sauce, and cornstarch to a saucepan.
2. Mix until there are no lumps of cornstarch.
3. Bring to a boil while stirring.
4. Simmer, add the minced garlic, and simmer until it reaches desired thickness.

## Notes
A thicker version of Chinese brown sauce.

Quick stovetop enchilada sauce built from toasted spices, tomato paste, and stock.

## Ingredients
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- 2 cups vegetable or chicken stock
- 2 tsp onion powder
- 1/2 tsp salt (to taste)
- 1 tsp garlic powder
- 3 tbsp chili powder
- 2 tsp cumin, ground
- 1 1/2 tsp oregano, dried
- 3 tbsp tomato paste

## Steps
1. Mix all spices together in a small bowl.
2. In a sauce pan, heat oil over medium-low heat.
3. Add spices and tomato paste. Whisk for 1 min to toast.
4. Slowly whisk in stock, continuing until all is incorporated.
5. Bring to a simmer for 5-10 minutes. Add salt as needed. @timer 5m
6. Cool completely before storing in a mason jar.

Homemade cinnamon rolls with a soft yeast dough, cinnamon-sugar filling, and cream cheese frosting.

## Ingredients
- 2 1/2 tsp yeast
- 1 cup warm milk
- 1 tsp salt
- 1/2 cup sugar
- 2 eggs (room temp)
- 0.333 cup margarine (melted)
- 4 1/2 cups bread flour
- 1 cup brown sugar (filling)
- 2 1/2 tbsp cinnamon, ground (filling)
- 0.333 cup butter, softened (filling)
- 3 oz cream cheese, softened (frosting)
- 1/4 cup butter, softened (frosting)
- 1 1/2 cups powdered sugar (frosting)
- 1/2 tsp vanilla extract (frosting)
- 1/8 tsp salt (frosting)

## Steps
1. **Make dough:** Dissolve yeast in warm milk in a large bowl. Mix in sugar, salt, eggs, and margarine. Add flour and mix well. Knead dough into a large ball.
2. **First rise:** Place in a large bowl, cover, and let rise in a warm place for 1 hour (doubling in size). @timer 1h
3. **Filling:** In a small bowl combine brown sugar and cinnamon.
4. **Shape rolls:** Roll dough into a 16 x 21" rectangle. Spread dough with 1/3 cup butter. Sprinkle with the cinnamon/sugar mixture. Roll up dough and cut into 12 rolls.
5. **Second rise:** Place rolls in a 9 x 13" glass baking pan. Cover and let rise ~30 minutes. @timer 30m
6. **Bake:** Preheat oven to 400°. Bake until golden brown, ~15 minutes. @timer 15m
7. **Frost:** Beat together the cream cheese, butter, powdered sugar, vanilla, and salt for the frosting. Spread frosting on warm rolls.

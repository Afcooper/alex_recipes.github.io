Baby spinach and strawberries tossed with a sweet sesame-poppy style dressing.

## Ingredients
- 1/2 cup sugar (dressing)
- 1/2 cup oil (dressing)
- 1/4 cup apple cider vinegar (dressing)
- 1/4 tsp Worcestershire sauce (dressing)
- 2 tbsp sesame seeds (dressing)
- 1 tbsp minced onion (dressing)
- 1/4 tsp paprika (dressing)
- 1 qt strawberries (salad)
- 12 oz baby spinach (salad)

## Steps
1. **Make dressing:** Combine the sugar, oil, apple cider vinegar, Worcestershire sauce, sesame seeds, minced onion, and paprika. Chill dressing for 1 hour. @timer 1h
2. **Toss:** Toss the dressing with the spinach and strawberries just before serving.

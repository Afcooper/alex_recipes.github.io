Baked wonton cups filled with a sweet and spicy cream cheese mixture.

## Ingredients
- 8 oz cream cheese, softened
- 4 green onions, sliced very thin (most white)
- 1 tsp Sriracha and sweet chili sauce (1+ tsp)
- 2 tbsp powdered sugar (or to taste)
- 1 pkg wonton wrappers (square or round)

## Steps
1. Preheat oven to 350-400°.
2. Mix all ingredients well in a bowl, except the wrappers.
3. **Bake cups:** Spray a mini muffin pan, place a wonton wrapper in each, and spray with butter spray. Bake until edges are brown, then remove from oven.
4. **Fill:** Put about a teaspoon of the mixture in the center of each wrapper.
5. **Warm:** Heat again for 2-3 minutes until the center mix is warm. Serve with favorite dipping sauce. @timer 3m

## Notes
Freeze on parchment uncooked for 2 hrs, then transfer to a freezer bag.

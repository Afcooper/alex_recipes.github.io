Spiced molasses ginger cookies rolled in granulated sugar with chocolate chips mixed in.

## Ingredients
- 3/4 cup sugar
- 1 tsp ginger
- 1 cup shortening
- 1/2 tsp ground cloves
- 1 egg
- 1 tsp cinnamon
- 1/4 cup Brer Rabbit molasses, dark
- 1 tsp soda
- 1/4 tsp salt
- 2 cups flour
-  | bag | chocolate chips

## Steps
1. Mix in order given.
2. Roll into walnut-size balls. Roll each ball in granulated sugar.
3. Place on ungreased cookie sheet.
4. Bake 10 minutes at 350ºF. @timer 10m

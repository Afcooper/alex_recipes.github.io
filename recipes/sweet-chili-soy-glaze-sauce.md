Glossy sweet chili and soy glaze cooked down with garlic and finished with green onion.

## Ingredients
- 1 tbsp olive oil
- 1 clove garlic (finely diced)
- 0.333 cup sweet chili sauce (Pantai)
- 3 tbsp soy sauce (light sodium, Kikkoman)
- 1 green onion (garnish)

## Steps
1. In a small saucepan, add olive oil over medium heat.
2. Add garlic, stirring, and cook 1 min until golden brown.
3. Add sweet chili sauce and soy sauce. Stir.
4. Cook until the sauce starts to bubble and thicken, semi-sticky.
5. Add to dish and garnish with green onion.

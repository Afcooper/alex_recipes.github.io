Oven-baked russet potato rubbed with oil and Kosher salt for a crisp, fluffy result.

## Ingredients
- Idaho russet potato
- high heat olive oil
- Kosher salt

## Steps
1. Wash potatoes and allow to dry completely.
2. Heat oven to 450°.
3. Poke 6-8 holes in each potato.
4. Drench in oil and rub all over.
5. Place on a plate and sprinkle Kosher salt very liberally all over.
6. Place on a baking sheet and cook until internal temp reaches 205° (45-60 min). @timer 45m
7. Remove excess salt.
8. Cut immediately in an 'X', then open and fluff.

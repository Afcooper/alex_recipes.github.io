Tiny twist pretzels tossed with ranch mix and buttery oil until the seasoning is fully absorbed.

## Ingredients
- 1 pkg tiny unsalted twist pretzels
- 1 pkg Ranch dressing mix
- 3/4 cup Orville Redenbacher buttery oil topping

## Steps
1. In a large bowl, cover pretzels with dressing mix.
2. Dribble oil over all and mix with a large spoon.
3. Mix every 15 minutes for at least 2 hours to absorb the oil. @timer 2h

Meatless meatballs made with Morning Star crumbles, baked until set.

## Ingredients
- 1 pkg Morning Star Crumbles
- 1 egg
- 1/2 medium sweet onion (yellow), small diced
- 1 tbsp Worcestershire sauce
- oregano
- salt & pepper
- 1/8 cup half and half

## Steps
1. Place crumbles in the microwave for 2 minutes (until thawed). Small dice the yellow onion. @timer 2m
2. Add all ingredients to a bowl and combine. Form into balls with a scoop and place on a parchment-lined cookie sheet.
3. Bake at 350F for about 15 minutes. @timer 15m

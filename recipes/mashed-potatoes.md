Creamy Yukon gold mashed potatoes with butter and cream cheese.

## Ingredients
- 5 lb Yukon gold potatoes, peeled & diced
- 2% or whole milk
- 6 tbsp butter
- 4 oz cream cheese
- salt

## Steps
1. Place potatoes in a pot, cover with water an inch or 2 above the potatoes, add a bit of Land O'Lakes butter or margarine (reduces the overspill) and salt.
2. Bring to a boil, then reduce to a simmer.
3. Simmer until cooked through (tender when pierced with a knife), about 12 minutes. @timer 12m
4. When done, drain in a colander.
5. Add butter and cream cheese to a large bowl, then add the potatoes and milk.
6. Mash together until smooth (use a mixer); can leave chunks.
7. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
8. Freeze extra in freezer-safe baggies, remove all air, and label.

## Notes
Freeze extra in freezer-safe baggies with all air removed, labeled.

Oven-baked bacon on parchment, with a method for freezing portions.

## Ingredients
- bacon

## Steps
1. Line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
2. Heat oven to 375˚.
3. Lay bacon flat without touching each other.
4. Bake for 15 minutes. @timer 15m

## Notes
To Freeze: Cut freezer paper or parchment paper strips. Lay 1 strip of bacon in each and roll up. Place all in a freezer bag.

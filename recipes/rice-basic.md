Basic stovetop white rice, rinsed and simmered until tender.

## Ingredients
- 1 cup uncooked white rice, well rinsed
- 1 1/4 cups water (1 1/4 - 1 1/2 cups)

## Steps
1. Combine the rinsed rice and water in a pot and bring to a boil. Cover, reduce the heat, and simmer for 15-25 minutes. @timer 25m
2. Remove from the heat and let it sit, covered, for 10 more minutes. Fluff with a fork. @timer 10m

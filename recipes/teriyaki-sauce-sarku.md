A copycat Sarku teriyaki sauce simmered with garlic and thickened with cornstarch, served over chicken and stir-fried vegetables.

## Ingredients
- 2 1/2 cups unsalted chicken stock
- 3/4 cup Kikkoman teriyaki sauce
- 1/4 cup Kikkoman low sodium soy sauce
- 2 cloves garlic
- 0.667 cup brown sugar
- 2 1/2 tbsp cornstarch
- 2 1/2 tbsp cold water
- chicken thighs
- cabbage mix
- broccoli
- yellow onion

## Steps
1. **Chicken:** Bake chicken thighs on a lined sheet in the oven with olive oil, salt, and pepper.
2. **Vegetables:** Stir fry the cabbage mix, broccoli, and yellow onion in a large skillet over high heat with olive oil and butter.
3. **Sauce:** First prepare the garlic in chopped pieces.
4. **Sauce:** Place the broth in a pot over medium heat and add the chopped garlic. Let the mixture simmer for about 10 minutes. @timer 10m
5. **Sauce:** Add the teriyaki sauce, then the soy sauce, then the brown sugar.
6. **Sauce:** Let this cook and reduce a bit, about 8-10 minutes. @timer 10m
7. **Sauce:** When the broth has simmered and cooked down a bit, remove the garlic with a slotted or mesh spoon.
8. **Sauce:** To thicken the broth, mix cornstarch and cold water until all lumps are dissolved. Add the slurry to the broth slowly, whisking vigorously for at least 1 minute.

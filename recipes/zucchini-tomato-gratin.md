Layered zucchini and tomatoes with garlic, basil, and Parmesan baked until bubbling.

## Ingredients
- 1 1/2 lb tomatoes (cut into ¼" thick slices)
- 2 medium zucchini (cut into 1/8" thick diagonal slices)
- 1 tbsp olive oil (plus 1 tsp)
- 4 cloves garlic, thinly sliced
- 1/4 cup thinly sliced basil leaves
- 3/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese

## Steps
1. **Drain tomatoes:** Drape tomato slices over a colander, sprinkle with salt, and let drain for 45 minutes. @timer 45m
2. **Sweat zucchini:** Spread zucchini on a baking sheet, sprinkle with salt, and let stand for 30 minutes to sweat out excess moisture. Rinse well and pat dry. @timer 30m
3. **Saute zucchini:** Preheat oven to 375. Heat 1 tsp oil in a nonstick skillet over medium-high heat. Saute zucchini 3-4 minutes or until golden brown. Transfer to a plate.
4. **Layer:** Layer ½ of the zucchini in an 8" square baking pan. Top with half of the tomatoes. Sprinkle with half of the garlic, half of the basil, and ¼ cup Parmesan; season with freshly ground pepper. Repeat with remaining zucchini, tomatoes, garlic, and basil. Drizzle the top with 1 tbsp oil and sprinkle with remaining ½ cup cheese.
5. **Bake:** Cover with foil and bake 10 minutes. Remove foil and bake 20 minutes more or until cheese is melted and gratin is bubbling. Let stand 5 minutes before serving. @timer 20m

## Notes
Nutrition (per serving): Calories 165, Protein 9g, Total fat 10g, Fiber 3g.

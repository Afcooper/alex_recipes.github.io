Cubed baguette tossed in seasoned garlic butter and baked crisp.

## Ingredients
- 4 cups cubed baguette
- 1/4 cup butter, melted
- 2 tsp Italian seasoning (4 tsp in 1 pkg)
- 1/2 tsp salt
- 1 1/2 tsp garlic powder

## Steps
1. Preheat oven to 375˚.
2. Line baking sheet with parchment paper.
3. Slice baguette into cubes.
4. In small bowl whisk melted butter and seasonings.
5. Toss bread cubes with seasoned butter.
6. Spread evenly on lined baking sheet.
7. Bake for 7-10 min. @timer 10m

## Notes
Store for up to 2 weeks.

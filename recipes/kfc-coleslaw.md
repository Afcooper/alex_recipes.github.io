Copycat KFC coleslaw with a creamy, sweet-tangy mayonnaise dressing.

## Ingredients
- 1/2 cup mayonnaise (Hellmans)
- 1/4 cup granulated sugar
- 1/4 cup milk + 3/4 tsp lemon (or buttermilk)
- 1 1/2 tbsp lemon juice
- 4 cups cabbage (about 1/2 head) finely shredded + carrot, or 15-16 oz shredded cabbage coleslaw bag
- 1/4 medium yellow onion (minced)
- 1 tsp salt
- 1/4 tsp black pepper
- 1 1/2 tbsp apple cider vinegar

## Steps
1. Shred cabbage and carrot in a food processor, then add the minced onion.
2. Combine all other ingredients in a large mixing bowl. Whisk together.
3. Combine the dressing and veggies.
4. Cover the bowl, place in the fridge, and marinate at least 4 hours, preferably overnight. @timer 12h

Mixed beans simmered with sauteed onions, bacon, mustard and Sweet Baby Ray's BBQ sauce.

## Ingredients
- 1 can butter beans or Great Northern beans
- 1 can chili beans w/ sauce
- 1 can pork 'n beans
- 1 can kidney beans
- 1 tbsp yellow mustard
- 1/2 cup Sweet Baby Ray's BBQ/Honey BBQ sauce
- 1 1/2 medium yellow onions, chopped
- 6 slices bacon (6–8 slices)

## Steps
1. Cook bacon in the oven or skillet, set aside.
2. Saute onions in a large skillet with oil until translucent and a bit brown on the edges.
3. Add drained butter and kidney beans, undrained pork 'n beans and chili beans, and reduce heat.
4. Add the other ingredients and stir.

## Notes
Can freeze within 3 days in a freezer bag or glass jar.

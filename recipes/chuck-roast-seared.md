A simple chuck roast slow-cooked over onions in beef broth until tender.

## Ingredients
- 1 1/2 lb chuck roast (1.5-1.75 lb)
- 1 small yellow onion, chopped in medium chunks
- 1 1/2 cups water or beef broth (BTB paste)
- salt and pepper

## Steps
1. Pat roast dry and leave out for 20 minutes (optional: sear brown in hot cast iron skillet). @timer 20m
2. Salt and pepper the roast.
3. Place onions on the bottom of the crock pot, then place roast on top of the onions.
4. Pour in water/broth.
5. Set to LOW for ~4 hours. @timer 4h

## Notes
Freeze leftovers in a freezer bag with some broth.

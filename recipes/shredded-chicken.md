Simply simmered chicken thighs shredded and moistened with reduced cooking broth.

## Ingredients
- 3 lb chicken thighs (skinless & boneless)
- 1 onion, medium (peeled and sliced)
- 4 cloves garlic
- 1 tsp salt
- 1/2 tsp ground black pepper

## Steps
1. In a heavy-bottomed saucepan combine all ingredients. Add just enough water to cover the chicken. Turn heat to high. Bring to a boil. Remove any scum or foam that floats to the top. Adjust heat to a gentle simmer. Cook for 30 minutes or until chicken is very tender and 175 degrees. @timer 30m
2. Transfer to cutting board, use 2 forks to shred the chicken.
3. After removing chicken, boil the liquid until it has reduced to 1/3rd. Spoon about 1 cup over the shredded chicken. Eat within 2-3 days.

## Notes
Chicken & broth will keep in fridge up to 3 days or frozen for 1 month.

Can be cooked in slow cooker on LOW for 4-6 hours.

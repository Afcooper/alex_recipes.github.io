Classic no-bake marshmallow and butter cereal treats cut into squares.

## Ingredients
- 3 tbsp butter
- 1 pkg miniature marshmallows (approx. 4 cups)
- 6 cups Kellogg's Rice Krispie cereal

## Steps
1. Melt butter in a saucepan over low heat.
2. Add marshmallows and stir until completely melted.
3. Remove from heat.
4. Add cereal and stir until well coated.
5. Pour into a buttered pan and cut into squares.

Creamy peanut butter frosting made with powdered sugar, butter, and milk.

## Ingredients
- 2 cups powdered sugar
- 1 stick butter (softened, unsalted)
- 1 tsp vanilla
- 1 cup creamy peanut butter
- 3 tbsp milk

## Steps
1. Beat the powdered sugar, softened butter, vanilla, and peanut butter together until smooth, adding milk to reach a spreadable consistency.

A creamy baked hashbrown casserole with two cream soups, sharp cheddar, and a crunchy cornflake topping.

## Ingredients
- 2 lb thawed frozen hashbrowns
- 1 tsp salt
- 1/4 tsp pepper
- 1 can cream of mushroom soup
- 1 can cream of celery soup
- 2 cups sharp cheddar cheese, shredded
- 1/2 cup melted butter
- 1/2 cup white onion (chopped)
- 2 cups crushed cornflakes (topping)
- 1/4 cup melted butter (topping)

## Steps
1. In a large mixing bowl, combine the hashbrowns, salt, pepper, cream of mushroom soup, cream of celery soup, cheddar cheese, melted butter, and white onion.
2. **Topping:** Mix together the crushed cornflakes and melted butter and sprinkle over the top.
3. Bake at 350º for 45 minutes to 1 hour. @timer 45m

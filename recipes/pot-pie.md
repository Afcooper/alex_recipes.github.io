A classic chicken pot pie with cream of chicken soup, mixed vegetables, and a double Pillsbury crust.

## Ingredients
- 3 chicken thighs (skin, bone-in)
- Pillsbury pie crusts
- frozen peas, corn, carrots
- 2 cans cream of chicken soup

## Steps
1. Coat chicken thighs with olive oil, salt, and pepper.
2. Bake at 400 degrees for 30 minutes or until done. Cool and cut into small pieces. @timer 30m
3. Cook peas, corn, and carrots in the microwave.
4. In a bowl, mix together the soup, veggies, and chicken.
5. Line the bottom of a pie pan with pie crust and pour in the mixture.
6. Cover with the top crust. Pinch top and bottom crusts together (optional: brush on egg wash). Cut slits in the top center.
7. Bake at 400 for 20-30 minutes. @timer 25m

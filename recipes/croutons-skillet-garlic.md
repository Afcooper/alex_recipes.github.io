Buttery garlic baguette cubes toasted in a skillet then baked until crispy.

## Ingredients
- 1/2 baguette (cut in small cubes)
- 3 tbsp butter (or more)
- 2 cloves garlic (minced)
- salt

## Steps
1. Preheat oven to 300˚ and line a baking sheet with parchment.
2. Melt butter in a large skillet over medium heat. Add garlic and stir for about 1 minute. Add salt and bread cubes and toss until thoroughly coated.
3. Transfer to the baking sheet in a single layer.
4. Bake in the oven 12-15 minutes until crispy. @timer 15m

No-bake white chocolate clusters with peanut butter, peanuts, marshmallows and Rice Krispies.

## Ingredients
- 12 oz white chocolate chips or vanilla almond bark (12-16 oz)
- 1/2 cup peanut butter
- 1/2 cup peanuts (1/2-3/4 c)
- 2 cups Rice Krispies cereal
- 1/2 cup miniature marshmallows

## Steps
1. Line a cookie sheet with parchment paper. Set aside.
2. Mix cereal, marshmallows (tear in half), and peanuts in a bowl.
3. Melt chocolate in the microwave, heating for 1 minute, then stirring, and repeat at 30-second intervals until melted.
4. Add peanut butter to the melted chocolate and stir.
5. Pour the melted chocolate and PB mixture over the cereal mixture; stir until well mixed.
6. Drop mixture using a tablespoon onto the prepared sheets.
7. Place in the refrigerator until set.

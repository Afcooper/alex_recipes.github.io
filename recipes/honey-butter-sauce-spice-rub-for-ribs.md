A whisked honey butter sauce paired with a paprika-cayenne spice rub for ribs.

## Ingredients
- 4 tbsp unsalted butter (melted) (honey butter)
- 1 tbsp brown sugar (honey butter)
- 1 tbsp honey (honey butter)
- 1/4 tsp cayenne pepper (honey butter)
- 1 1/2 tsp paprika (spice rub)
- 1/2 tsp cumin (spice rub)
- 1 tsp garlic powder (spice rub)
- 1/2 tsp onion powder (spice rub)
- 1/2 tsp cayenne pepper (spice rub)
- 1/4 tsp ground mustard (spice rub)

## Steps
1. **Honey Butter:** Whisk the melted butter, brown sugar, honey, and cayenne pepper all together.
2. **Spice Rub:** Combine the paprika, cumin, garlic powder, onion powder, cayenne pepper, and ground mustard.

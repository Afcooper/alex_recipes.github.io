A fresh chunky salsa of corn, black beans or edamame, tomatoes, and cilantro.

## Ingredients
- red onion
- corn
- edamame or black beans
- tomatoes
- cilantro
- salt and pepper
- garlic powder

## Steps
1. **Combine:** Chop the red onion, tomatoes, and cilantro. Combine with corn and edamame or black beans. Season with salt, pepper, and garlic powder to taste.

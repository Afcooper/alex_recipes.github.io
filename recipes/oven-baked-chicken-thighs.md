Crispy skin-on, bone-in chicken thighs baked skin-side down then flipped to finish.

## Ingredients
- chicken thighs (skin on, bone in)
- salt or other seasonings

## Steps
1. Thaw chicken thighs in refrigerator overnight.
2. Trim excess fat and skin with scissors. Let sit out for about 20 minutes. Pat dry with paper towels.
3. Place on a rimmed cookie sheet in oven. Heat oven to 350 degrees.
4. Poke skin side multiple times with the tip of a sharp knife. Salt and season each side.
5. Place thighs skin side down on sheet pan. Bake at 350-375 degrees for 25 minutes. Flip and bake an additional 10 minutes. Safe to eat at a temp of 175 degrees. @timer 25m

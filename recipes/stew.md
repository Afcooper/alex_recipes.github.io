A slow-cooker beef and cabbage stew with fire-roasted tomatoes, celery, and onion.

## Ingredients
- 3 tbsp Better than Bouillon Roasted Beef paste
- 8 cups hot water (to mix with the beef paste)
- 2 cups chicken broth (or 14 oz can)
- 1 can fire-roasted whole tomatoes (28 oz, drained, cut into chunks)
- 1 can fire-roasted tomatoes (14.5 oz, drained, cut into chunks)
- 3 celery stalks (3-5)
- 1 medium onion - white, yellow or vidalia (optional: sauté prior)
- 1 head green cabbage (chopped in large pieces)
- 3 tbsp butter (3-4 T)

## Steps
1. Chop the onion and celery into pieces.
2. Add the chopped cabbage, tomatoes, onion, salt, pepper, butter, and broth to the slow cooker.
3. Let simmer for 4-5 hours on low in the slow cooker. With 2 hours left, add the celery. @timer 4.5h

Baked Stove Top stuffing enriched with onion, egg, half and half, chicken stock, and optional oysters.

## Ingredients
- 1 box Stove Top Chicken stuffing
- 1 stick butter (melt 6 T)
- 1 medium sweet yellow onion (chopped)
- 1 egg
- 1/2 cup Half N Half
- 3/4 cup chicken stock
- 1 pt oysters (optional)

## Steps
1. Preheat oven to 350.
2. Heat 2 T butter in a skillet and saute onions until translucent.
3. Mix all ingredients (use the Stove Top unprepared) in a large mixing bowl.
4. Transfer to a buttered 8x8 pan.
5. Bake at 350 for 35-40 minutes. @timer 35m

A glossy corn-syrup glaze icing for decorating sugar cookies, tinted with gel food coloring.

## Ingredients
- 3 cups powdered sugar (sifted)
- 3 tbsp milk (3–4 tbsp)
- 2 tbsp light corn syrup
- 1/2 tsp vanilla
- gel food coloring

## Steps
1. If frosting is too thick add additional milk (1 tsp at a time).
2. If frosting is too thin add more powdered sugar.

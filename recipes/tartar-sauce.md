A creamy mayonnaise-based tartar sauce with sweet pickles, lemon, and Dijon.

## Ingredients
- 1/2 cup mayonnaise
- 1/8 cup sweet pickles (diced finely)
- 1/2 tbsp lemon juice (fresh squeezed)
- 1/2 tsp Dijon mustard
- 1/4 tsp onion powder
- 1/4 tsp black pepper
- Worcestershire sauce (dash)

## Steps
1. Combine in a medium bowl and whisk together: mayonnaise, pickles, lemon juice, mustard, onion powder, and pepper.
2. Chill in the refrigerator. This improves the flavor significantly.

Oatmeal-bound beef meatballs baked then finished in BBQ sauce.

## Ingredients
- 1 lb ground beef
- 3/4 cup oatmeal
- onion (as desired)
- 1 1/2 tsp salt
- 1/4 tsp pepper
- 1 cup milk
- BBQ sauce

## Steps
1. Mix and form into walnut-size balls.
2. Place on an aluminum foil lined and sprayed baking sheet.
3. Bake at 350 degrees for 20 minutes. @timer 20m
4. Cover with BBQ sauce and bake an additional 30 minutes. @timer 30m

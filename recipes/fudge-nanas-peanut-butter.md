Nana's brown sugar peanut butter fudge with marshmallow creme, beaten smooth and cut into squares.

## Ingredients
- 1 cup sugar
- 1 cup brown sugar
- 1/2 cup evaporated milk
- 1 tbsp butter
- 3/4 cup peanut butter
- 1 pt marshmallow creme

## Steps
1. Mix sugars, milk, and butter in a 2-quart pan.
2. Bring to a boil and cook for 5 minutes, stirring constantly. @timer 5m
3. Remove from heat and add peanut butter and marshmallow creme.
4. Beat until smooth and pour into a buttered square pan.
5. Cool. Cut into square pieces.

Spiced hot apple cider simmered with allspice, cloves, and cinnamon, garnished with clove-studded oranges.

## Ingredients
- 1 gal apple juice
- 0.667 cup sugar
- 2 tsp allspice
- 2 tsp whole cloves
- cinnamon sticks (2-3")
- 2 oranges (studded with cloves)

## Steps
1. Boil all ingredients except the oranges.
2. Simmer for 20 minutes. @timer 20m
3. Strain.
4. Pour into a punch bowl and float the clove-studded oranges on top.

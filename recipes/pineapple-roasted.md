Sliced pineapple roasted until beginning to brown and caramelized.

## Ingredients
- pineapple
- canola oil (spray)

## Steps
1. Slice pineapple.
2. Cover baking sheet with parchment.
3. Heat oven to 400˚.
4. Place on baking sheet.
5. Spray lightly with canola oil.
6. Bake 15 minutes. @timer 15m
7. Flip.
8. Spray lightly again (optional).
9. Bake another 15 minutes or until beginning to brown. @timer 15m
10. Let cool.

Spaghetti tossed with a no-cook sauce of anchovies, garlic, chili, and parmesan.

## Ingredients
- 1/4 lb spaghetti (¼-½ lb)
- 2 oz anchovies
- 3 cloves garlic, minced
- 1/2 serrano or jalapeño
- 2 tbsp parmesan, grated
- 2 tbsp olive oil

## Steps
1. Cook spaghetti. Drain, reserving ½ cup pasta water.
2. In a bowl, combine the oil of the anchovies, finely diced anchovies, minced garlic, grated parmesan, and olive oil. Microwave for 1-1.5 minutes before serving.
3. Add spaghetti to the sauce, stir, adding some extra pasta water if needed, and garnish with parmesan.

A sweet-tangy plum jam glaze with soy sauce and balsamic, thickened with cornstarch.

## Ingredients
- 3/4 cup plum jam
- 1 cup water
- 2 tbsp brown sugar (2-3 T)
- 1 tbsp soy sauce
- 3 tbsp balsamic vinegar
- 2 tbsp cornstarch

## Steps
1. Combine plum jam, water, brown sugar, soy sauce, and balsamic vinegar in a saucepan.
2. Stir in cornstarch and cook, stirring, until the glaze thickens.

A layered Tex-Mex casserole of ground beef, refried beans, enchilada sauce, and Fritos.

## Ingredients
- 1 lb ground beef
- 1 can enchilada sauce (mild)
- 1 can refried beans
- 1 pkg Fritos
- lettuce, tomatoes & shredded cheddar (optional)

## Steps
1. Brown the meat. Drain.
2. Heat the enchilada sauce and beans.
3. Cover the bottom of a casserole dish with Fritos.
4. Add meat, then beans, then sauce.
5. Cover with lettuce, tomatoes, and cheese.

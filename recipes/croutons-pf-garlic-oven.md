Pepperidge Farm bread cubes tossed in garlic butter and olive oil, then baked crisp.

## Ingredients
- 7 slices Butter Farmhouse Pepperidge Farm bread
- 3 tbsp olive oil
- 4 tbsp butter
- 1 tbsp garlic powder
- 1/2 tsp salt

## Steps
1. **Prep:** Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Line a baking sheet with parchment paper. Slice PF Butter bread into 3/4-inch cubes and place in a large bowl.
2. **Coat:** In another bowl combine olive oil and melted butter. Slowly drizzle butter mix over bread cubes, tossing and stirring. Sprinkle with garlic powder and salt to coat.
3. **Bake:** Spread in a single layer on the baking sheet. Bake 10-15 min. @timer 15m

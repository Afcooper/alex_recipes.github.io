Creamy mashed cauliflower blended with cream cheese, sour cream and cheddar.

## Ingredients
- 1 head cauliflower
- 1/2 cup shredded cheddar cheese
- 15 oz vegetable broth (Full Circle) or water
- 1/4 cup green onions, white & green parts, sliced (optional)
- 2 oz cream cheese
- salt & pepper to taste
- 0.333 cup sour cream

## Steps
1. Place cauliflower florets in a large saucepan. Add broth and bring to a boil. Cover and simmer until very tender, about 20 minutes. @timer 20m
2. Drain thoroughly through a fine sieve. Mash cauliflower in a bowl with a fork or hand masher.
3. Add cream cheese, sour cream, cheddar cheese, salt and pepper. Use a mixer to blend to desired consistency. Reheat before serving.

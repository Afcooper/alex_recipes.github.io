Baked layered bean and cheese dip topped with guacamole, tomatoes and green onions for chips.

## Ingredients
- 1 can refried beans (16 oz)
- 2 tomatoes, diced
- 1/2 cup sour cream
- 1 bunch green onions
- 1 pkg taco seasoning
- cilantro
- 1/4 cup cheddar cheese, shredded
- 3/4 cup pepper jack cheese, shredded
- guacamole (avocado, red onion, salt, garlic powder, lemon)

## Steps
1. Preheat oven to 375°. Line an approx. 8x8 baking dish with oil.
2. Mix refried beans, cheese, sour cream and taco mix in a bowl.
3. Spread bean mixture evenly to the bottom of the pan. Bake for 25 min, or until cheese is bubbly and slightly browned on the edges. @timer 25m
4. Garnish with guacamole, green onions, cilantro, tomatoes and chips.

Homemade soft caramels cooked with brown sugar, corn syrup, butter, and sweetened condensed milk.

## Ingredients
- 2 1/4 cups dark brown sugar
- 1 tsp vanilla
- 1 cup light corn syrup
- 1 cup butter (2 sticks)
- 14 oz sweetened condensed milk
- 1/4 tsp salt

## Steps
1. Line a 9x13 pan with parchment paper. Spray with vegetable oil.
2. Melt the butter in a non-stick sauce pan over medium heat.
3. Add the sugar and salt. Stir. Stir in the corn syrup. Gradually add the milk, stirring constantly.
4. Continue to stir over medium heat to 240-245º, about 12 minutes. @timer 12m
5. Remove from heat. Stir in the vanilla. Pour into the pan and let cool.
6. Place in the fridge. Take out and cut ¾" lines vertically, then horizontally into 5 rows. Wrap in wax paper (cut to 4x5"). Yields 60+.

## Notes
Helpful tools: digital thermometer, long wood spoon, rubber spatula.

Yields 60+ caramels.

Slow-cooker chicken thighs in honey BBQ sauce, shredded for sandwiches or bowls.

## Ingredients
- 4 chicken thighs (skin and fat removed)
- 1/2 onion, medium (grated with juices)
- 1 1/2 cups Sweet Baby Ray's Honey BBQ Sauce
- 2 tbsp light brown sugar
- 1 tsp olive oil
- 1 tsp garlic powder
- 1/4 tsp paprika
- 1/4 tsp chili powder
- 1/4 tsp black or white pepper
- 1 tbsp Worcestershire sauce

## Steps
1. **Add chicken:** Pat chicken dry with paper towels. Place chicken in slow cooker on Low.
2. **Make sauce:** In a bowl, combine all other ingredients and stir to combine. Pour the BBQ sauce mixture into the slow cooker over the chicken.
3. **Cook:** Cover and cook on Low (6-7 hrs), or High (3-4 hrs). @timer 6h
4. **Shred:** When chicken is tender and can pull apart easily, it is ready to shred. Shred chicken and mix the BBQ sauce together.

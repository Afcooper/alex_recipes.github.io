Nana's classic roll-and-cut sugar cookies, perfect for decorating on cookie day.

## Ingredients
- 1.333 cups shortening
- 1 1/2 cups sugar
- 1 tsp vanilla
- 2 eggs
- 8 tsp milk
- 4 cups flour
- 3 tsp baking powder
- 1/2 tsp salt

## Steps
1. Mix the shortening and sugar. Add the vanilla, eggs, and milk.
2. In a separate bowl, mix the flour, baking powder, and salt.
3. Add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients.
4. Roll and cut.
5. Bake at 375º on an ungreased cookie sheet for 7-10 minutes. @timer 10m

Classic rolled sugar cookies pressed flat with a sugar-dipped glass and baked.

## Ingredients
- 1 cup sugar
- 1 tsp baking soda
- 1 cup shortening
- 1 tsp cream of tartar
- 1/2 cup margarine/butter
- 1/2 tsp salt
- 1 egg
- 2 1/2 cups flour
- 1 tsp vanilla

## Steps
1. Mix wet ingredients; mix dry ingredients separately. Gradually add the dry to the wet ingredients.
2. Roll dough into balls.
3. Press down with a glass dipped in sugar.
4. Bake at 350 degrees for 8 to 10 minutes. @timer 10m

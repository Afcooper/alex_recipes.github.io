Rich creamed corn with cream cheese and butter, made in the crockpot or on the stovetop.

## Ingredients
- 20 oz frozen corn (20-30 oz, ~5 cups / 8-9 ears sweet corn from cob)
- 8 oz cream cheese
- 1/4 cup butter
- 1/4 cup milk + half and half
- 1 tbsp sugar (optional)
- 1 tsp salt (or to taste)
- 1/2 tsp pepper (or to taste)

## Steps
1. **Crockpot method:** Put all the ingredients in the crockpot. Cook on high 2-4 hours or low 4-6 hours. Stir after about an hour. @timer 2h
2. **Stovetop method:** Alternatively, cook on the stovetop in a dutch oven or deep skillet until corn is tender and butter and cream cheese are melted (~20-30 min). @timer 20m

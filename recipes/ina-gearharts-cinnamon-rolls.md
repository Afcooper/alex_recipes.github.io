Soft yeast-raised cinnamon rolls rolled with butter, sugar and cinnamon and finished with buttercream frosting.

## Ingredients
- 2 cups milk
- 2 pkg dry yeast
- 1/4 cup butter
- 1/2 cup warm water
- 2 eggs
- 1/4 cup sugar
- 1 tsp salt
- 5 cups flour (5–6 c)
- 1/2 cup sugar (for filling)
- 1 1/2 tsp cinnamon (for filling)
- raisins (optional)
- buttercream frosting

## Steps
1. **Dough:** Scald milk. Add butter to milk. Cool. Add sugar and yeast to warm water until dissolved. Mix all together.
2. **Dough:** Stir in as much flour as you can with a spoon. Place on a floured board and knead in the rest of the flour. Knead well until a soft, shiny ball is formed.
3. **Rise:** Place in a lightly greased bowl. Grease top of dough. Cover with waxed paper and cloth. Place in a warm spot. Let double in size, punch down, let rise again.
4. **Shape & fill:** Roll dough out. Spread with butter. Sprinkle generously with sugar and cinnamon, add raisins (optional). Roll up, pinch together. Cut 1" thick. Place on pan, let rise.
5. **Bake:** Bake at 400° until golden. Make buttercream frosting.

Cake-mix red velvet cookies topped with whipped cream cheese frosting.

## Ingredients
- 1 box red velvet cake mix (Duncan Hines)
- 0.333 cup oil
- 2 eggs
- 4 tbsp butter, softened (frosting)
- 4 oz cream cheese (frosting)
- 2 cups powdered sugar (frosting)
- 1 tsp vanilla (frosting)

## Steps
1. **Cookies:** Preheat oven to 350. Line cookie sheet with parchment. With electric mixer beat eggs, oil, and cake mix.
2. **Cookies:** Roll into balls. Place on cookie sheet, flatten with palm of hand.
3. **Cookies:** Bake for 10 min. @timer 10m
4. **Cream Cheese Frosting:** Whip everything but powdered sugar first (butter, cream cheese, vanilla). Add powdered sugar.

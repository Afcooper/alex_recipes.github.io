Thin baguette slices brushed with garlic butter and baked until crisp.

## Ingredients
- 1 Panera baguette
- 1/2 cup butter
- salt
- 6 cloves large garlic cloves (minced)

## Steps
1. Preheat oven to 350. Line baking sheet with parchment paper.
2. Slice baguette into 1/8" thin slices.
3. Lay slices in single layer on cookie sheet.
4. Heat butter in microwave.
5. Mince garlic, the smaller the better.
6. Add minced garlic and salt to butter, mix.
7. Using a pastry brush, brush butter mixture on each slice.
8. Bake in oven ~7 minutes, store chips in airtight container. @timer 7m

A hearty soup of seared chicken thighs simmered with onion, celery and beef-boosted broth.

## Ingredients
- 3 chicken thighs (30 min on counter from fridge)
- 1 yellow onion (small, chopped)
- 4 stalks celery stalks (4-5, chopped)
- 32 oz chicken broth, organic
- 1 1/2 tbsp Better Than Bouillon Roasted Beef base
- salt & pepper

## Steps
1. Line a baking sheet with foil. Heat oven to 375.
2. Pat chicken dry and season with salt & pepper. Heat a dutch oven to medium-high on the stovetop, add ~1 tbsp olive oil, and wait until it shimmers.
3. Add chicken skin side down. Leave alone for 5 minutes, then flip and cook ~2-3 minutes. Place on baking sheet in oven until temp reaches 175, ~15 minutes. @timer 15m
4. Scrape fond on bottom of dutch oven. Add onions and cook ~2-3 minutes until opaque. Add celery and cook another 2 minutes.
5. Add beef bouillon to 2 cups hot water and mix thoroughly. Add chicken broth and the bouillon mixture.
6. After reaching a simmer, add chopped chicken thighs (salt & pepper them first).

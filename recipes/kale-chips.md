Crispy oven-baked kale chips tossed with popcorn seasoning and salt.

## Ingredients
- 1 bunch kale
- vegetable or canola oil
- popcorn seasoning (Sour Cream & Onion or Ranch)
- salt

## Steps
1. Wash kale bunch, shake off moisture, lay to dry on paper towels, pat dry.
2. Preheat oven to 350. Line parchment on cookie sheet.
3. When dry, rip off large pieces from main stem just enough to eat that day.
4. Place in large bowl, drizzle with about 1 tbsp oil. Massage thoroughly so oil is on all pieces, but not soggy; add more kale if it is too soggy.
5. Sprinkle popcorn seasoning and a bit of salt, mix with hands until mixed well. Repeat until desired flavor.
6. Place kale pieces spaced apart on single layer.
7. Bake for about 10 minutes. @timer 10m

## Notes
3 stalks yield ~2 batches.

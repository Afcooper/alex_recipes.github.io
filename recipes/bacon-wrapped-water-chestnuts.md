Bacon-wrapped water chestnuts baked and glazed in a sweet-tangy ketchup sauce.

## Ingredients
- 2 cans whole water chestnuts, drained
- 1 1/2 lb bacon slices
- 1/2 cup sugar
- 1/4 cup vinegar
- 1 cup catsup
- 1/4 tsp celery seed

## Steps
1. Wrap bacon around each chestnut. Use toothpicks to hold.
2. Bake at 350° for 35 minutes. Drain grease. @timer 35m
3. Mix the last 4 ingredients (sugar, vinegar, catsup, celery seed) and pour over chestnuts. Bake 20 minutes. @timer 20m

A fresh avocado dip with red onion, cilantro, lime, and jalapeño.

## Ingredients
- 3 avocados
- 1/4 cup red onion (finely diced)
- 1/4 cup cilantro (chopped)
- 1 tbsp lime or lemon juice (1-2 tbsp)
- 1/2 jalapeño, finely minced (optional)
- 1/2 tsp salt or more to taste
- 1/2 tsp garlic powder
- black pepper, pinch

## Steps
1. Mash avocados, then add remaining ingredients.
2. Cover with plastic wrap to prevent browning. Refrigerate.

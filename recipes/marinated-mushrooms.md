White button mushrooms boiled then steeped in a tangy vinegar-garlic marinade overnight.

## Ingredients
- 1/2 cup white vinegar (marinade)
- 2 tsp salt (marinade)
- 1 tsp sugar (marinade)
- 3 cloves garlic, finely chopped (marinade)
- 1/2 tsp ground pepper (marinade)
- 1/2 jar pimentos (or 1 red pepper chopped) (marinade)
- 1 tsp oregano, ground, or dill (1-2 tsp) (marinade)
- 2 cups water (marinade)
- 1/2 cup vegetable or sunflower oil (marinade)
- 2 lb white button mushrooms (quartered or halved)
- 16 cups water (for boiling mushrooms)
- 3 tbsp vinegar (for boiling mushrooms)

## Steps
1. **Make the marinade first:** In a medium saucepan, combine vinegar, salt, sugar, garlic, bay leaves, ground black pepper, red pepper and oregano. Simmer marinade for 5 minutes. @timer 5m
2. **Finish marinade:** Add 2 cups water, bring to a boil and simmer another 5 minutes. Lastly, add oil and turn off the heat. @timer 5m
3. **Boil water:** Boil 16 cups (4 quarts) water with 3 Tbsp vinegar (or juice from 1 lemon) to prevent browning of mushrooms.
4. **Cook mushrooms:** Rinse mushrooms (immediately after rinsing, cut into quarters, halves, or even whole if small) and place in boiling water. Stir, cover and cook 3 minutes. @timer 3m
5. **Marinate:** Drain mushrooms and pour marinade over them. Boil another 2-3 minutes in the marinade. @timer 3m
6. **Chill:** Cool to room temp and refrigerate overnight, then enjoy for 1.5 months sealed in a jar in the refrigerator. @timer 12h

## Notes
Keeps up to 1.5 months sealed in a jar in the refrigerator.

Tender baby carrots glazed in a brown sugar, butter, and Dijon mustard sauce.

## Ingredients
- 2 lb baby carrots
- 1/4 tsp salt
- 1/2 cup packed light brown sugar
- 3 tbsp butter
- 2 tbsp Dijon mustard
- 1/4 tsp white pepper

## Steps
1. Put carrots in a saucepan, sprinkle with salt, and cover with water. Bring to a boil, cover with a lid, and simmer on low until tender. Drain.
2. In the saucepan put the remaining ingredients, stir, and add the carrots.
3. Stir over low heat until well coated.

Hearty beef chili with fire-roasted tomatoes, kidney beans and warm spices, made for freezing.

## Ingredients
- 1 tbsp olive oil
- 1 tbsp garlic powder
- 1 yellow onion, medium (diced)
- 1 1/2 tsp salt
- 3/4 lb ground beef 80-85%
- 1/2 tsp pepper
- 2 1/2 tbsp chili powder
- 1/4 tsp cayenne pepper
- 2 tbsp cumin
- 1 1/2 cups beef broth / Better Than Bouillon roast beef
- 1 tbsp sugar
- 2 cans fire-roasted diced tomatoes (15 oz each)
- 2 tbsp tomato paste
- 8 oz tomato sauce (8–15 oz)
- 16 oz red kidney or black beans (drained & rinsed)

## Steps
1. Add olive oil to a large pot over medium-high heat for 2 min. @timer 2m
2. Add onion, cook for 3-5 min, stirring occasionally.
3. Add beef to pot. Break apart with a wooden spoon. Cook about 6 min. @timer 6m
4. Add chili powder, cumin, sugar, tomato paste, garlic powder, salt, pepper and cayenne. Stir.
5. Add broth, diced tomatoes with juice, beans and tomato sauce. Stir. Bring to a low boil, reduce heat to low/medium-low and cook uncovered for 20-25 min, stirring occasionally. @timer 25m
6. Freeze leftovers in quart freezer bags.

## Notes
Makes 6-8 servings.

Butter lettuce salad with sweet corn, kidney beans, tomatoes, and craisins tossed in creamy balsamic.

## Ingredients
- butter lettuce
- sweet corn
- craisins (low sugar)
- kidney beans (strain, rinse)
- tomatoes
- balsamic dressing (Brianna's Homestyle Creamy Balsamic New American)
- oranges (optional)

## Steps
1. Strain and rinse the kidney beans.
2. Combine butter lettuce, sweet corn, craisins, kidney beans, and tomatoes in a bowl. Add oranges if desired.
3. Toss with creamy balsamic dressing.

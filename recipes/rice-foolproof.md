Foolproof stove-top long grain white rice cooked, rested, and fluffed.

## Ingredients
- 1 1/4 cups water (1 1/4 to 1/2 cup)
- 1 cup long grain white rice (do NOT rinse)

## Steps
1. Place water and rice in a saucepan, stir, and bring to a simmer (small bubbles boiling gently).
2. Turn heat down and cover with lid.
3. Cook for 13 minutes. Do not stir or remove lid. Water should now be absorbed; tilt to check. @timer 13m
4. Rest: remove from stove with lid on for 10 minutes. @timer 10m
5. Fluff: remove lid, fluff and serve.

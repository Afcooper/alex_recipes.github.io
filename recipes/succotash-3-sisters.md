Butter beans and fresh corn sauteed with onion and red pepper, finished with lemon.

## Ingredients
- 16 oz butter beans & liquid
- 3 ears corn (cut from cob)
- 1/2 red pepper (1/4" diced)
- 1 onion, yellow, small-medium (finely diced)
- 3 tbsp butter
- garlic
- cayenne pepper
- lemon

## Steps
1. Drain beans over a bowl, reserving the liquid. Rinse beans with cold water.
2. In a 12" non-stick skillet over medium-high heat, melt butter, then add onion and red pepper. Cook 4-5 min, stirring frequently, until slightly brown.
3. Add garlic and cayenne pepper. Cook 30 sec.
4. Reduce heat to medium. Add butter beans and corn. Cook 4 min, stirring frequently. @timer 4m
5. Add butter bean liquid (2 tbsp to 1/4 cup) and squeeze in lemon.

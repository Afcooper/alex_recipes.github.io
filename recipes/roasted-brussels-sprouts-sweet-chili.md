Brussels sprouts roasted until nearly charred, then tossed with sweet chili sauce.

## Ingredients
- Brussels sprouts (cut ends, halve or quarter)
- oil
- garlic powder
- onion powder
- paprika
- salt/pepper
- Lawry's season salt
- sweet chili sauce (to your liking)

## Steps
1. **Prep:** Cut ends off Brussels sprouts and halve or quarter them. Coat in oil and seasonings (garlic powder, onion powder, paprika, salt/pepper, Lawry's season salt).
2. **Roast:** Spread in an even layer on a baking sheet. Roast in oven at 400 degrees until they look almost burnt (20-30 min), tossing halfway through. @timer 30m
3. **Finish:** Remove from oven, place in a bowl and add sweet chili sauce to your liking.

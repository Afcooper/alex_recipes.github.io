A savory portabella mushroom soup built on beef and chicken stock with sauteed onion, carrot and celery.

## Ingredients
- 2 tbsp butter
- 1 cup onion, chopped
- 1/2 cup carrot
- 1 cup celery
- 1 tbsp garlic, minced
- 8 oz Portabella mushrooms (2)
- red pepper flakes (dash)
- 3 cups beef stock
- 2 cups chicken stock
- 2 tbsp soy sauce
- 1/2 tsp worcestershire sauce
- salt & pepper

## Steps
1. Melt butter in a stock pot.
2. Add onion, carrot and celery; cook over medium heat until translucent.
3. Add garlic and mushrooms. Cook 5 minutes on medium-low heat. @timer 5m
4. Add stocks, worcestershire sauce and red pepper flakes. Season with salt and pepper.
5. Reduce to low and simmer for 30 minutes. @timer 30m

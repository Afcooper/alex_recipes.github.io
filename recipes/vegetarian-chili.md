A hearty meatless chili made with Morning Star crumbles, beans, and vegetables in the crock pot.

## Ingredients
- Morning Star Crumbles
- 1 can chili beans in sauce
- 1 can red kidney beans
- 14 oz diced fire roasted tomatoes
- 1/2 red onion (chopped)
- 1 carrot (chopped)
- 1 zucchini (chopped)
- 1/2 cup edamame
- 1/2 cup corn
- 1 pkg chili mix
- garlic powder
- chili powder
- 1 tsp sugar

## Steps
1. Combine all ingredients and cook in a crock pot for about 2 hours on low. @timer 2h
2. Add sour cream or sharp cheddar cheese to individual bowls.

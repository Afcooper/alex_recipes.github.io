A glossy stir-fry style brown sauce thickened with cornstarch from beef broth, oyster and soy sauce.

## Ingredients
- 1 1/2 cups beef broth
- 1 1/2 tbsp oyster sauce
- 2 tsp soy sauce (low sodium)
- 2 tsp sugar
- 1 1/4 tbsp cornstarch (corn flour or potato starch)

## Steps
1. Add broth, oyster sauce, soy sauce and cornstarch to a saucepan.
2. Mix until no lumps of cornstarch remain.
3. Bring to a boil while stirring, then simmer until sauce reaches desired thickness.

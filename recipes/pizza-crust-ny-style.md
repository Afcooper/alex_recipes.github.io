A bread-flour New York style pizza dough that chills at least 24 hours before shaping into four crusts.

## Ingredients
- 1 3/4 cups water (lukewarm)
- 2 tsp active dry yeast (1 pkg)
- 4 1/2 cups bread flour
- 1 1/2 tsp kosher salt
- 1 tbsp olive oil

## Steps
1. In a large bowl, whisk water and yeast; let sit for 10 minutes. @timer 10m
2. Gradually add flour and salt, stopping when the dough is slightly sticky.
3. Sprinkle 2 T flour on the surface and knead the dough with both hands until it bounces back slowly to the touch (about 5 min). @timer 5m
4. Shape dough into a ball, transfer to a bowl greased with 1 T olive oil, cover with plastic, and chill in the fridge for at least 24 hrs. @timer 24h
5. An hour before baking, take the dough out and cover at room temperature. @timer 1h
6. Divide into 4 equal pieces. Shape and flatten each to a disk.
7. Roll dough on parchment sprinkled with cornmeal, or a combo of cornmeal and spray oil (about 8" diameter).
8. Transfer from parchment to a cornmeal-sprayed dark baking sheet.
9. Assemble with sauce and other ingredients.

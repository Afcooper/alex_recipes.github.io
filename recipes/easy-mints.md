Quick almond-flavored mints kneaded smooth and molded or sliced.

## Ingredients
- 1/4 cup butter
- 2 tbsp water
- 1/4 tsp salt
- 1/2 tsp almond extract
- 3 1/2 cups powdered sugar
- food coloring (optional)

## Steps
1. Combine butter and water. Place over low heat until butter melts. Remove from heat.
2. Add salt and extract.
3. Gradually add sugar.
4. Knead until smooth.
5. Mold into individual candies or form small rolls to slice.

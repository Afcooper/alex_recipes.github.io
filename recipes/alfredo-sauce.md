A lighter Alfredo sauce with Parmesan, sour cream, spinach, and nutritional yeast for pasta.

## Ingredients
- spray butter
- 1/2 cup freshly grated Parmesan
- 1/2 cup sour cream, fat free
- chopped spinach
- 1 clove freshly grated garlic (1-2 cloves)
- nutritional yeast (Braggs for B12)

## Steps
1. Use spray butter (or real butter) to generously coat a fry pan. On low to medium, heat spinach, then add garlic. Remove from heat.
2. Stir in sour cream and Parmesan. Add a little pasta water from boiled linguine (boil quinoa linguine with oil for 7 minutes) to the cream sauce. Stir in nutritional yeast.

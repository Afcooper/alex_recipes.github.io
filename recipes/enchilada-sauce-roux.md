A homemade red enchilada sauce built from a spiced roux and chicken broth.

## Ingredients
- 2 tbsp butter
- 1 tbsp vegetable oil
- 3 tbsp flour
- 1 1/2 tbsp chili powder
- 2 tbsp paprika
- 1 tsp onion powder
- 2 tsp garlic powder
- 1 tsp cumin
- 3 cups chicken broth
- 1/2 tsp Mexican oregano
- 2 tsp tomato paste
- 1 1/2 tsp chicken bouillon
- 1/2 tsp sugar
- 1/2 tsp salt or to taste

## Steps
1. In a deep skillet on medium-low heat, melt butter, add oil, then add flour and make a roux until golden brown, stirring constantly.
2. Add each spice from chili powder to cumin one at a time to the skillet on low, then add ½ of the chicken broth. Stir constantly. Use a whisk or rubber spatula.
3. Add oregano, tomato paste, bouillon, sugar, and salt, adding broth throughout. Let simmer 10-25 minutes. @timer 25m

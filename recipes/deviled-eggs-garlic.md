Classic deviled eggs with mayo, mustard, butter and garlic powder, garnished with paprika and chives.

## Ingredients
- eggs
- mayonnaise
- mustard + pinch of Dijon
- 1 tbsp softened butter
- sugar
- garlic powder
- salt

## Steps
1. Boil eggs for 5 minutes on medium-high, then take off heat for 12 minutes. Run under cold water. Tap the ends first, then the sides to loosen the shells. Start at the hollowest end and dip in water to loosen the membrane. @timer 5m
2. Cut eggs in half, place yolks in a bowl and mash with a fork.
3. Add remaining ingredients and fill the egg halves.
4. Garnish with paprika, pepper and chives (optional).

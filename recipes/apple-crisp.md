Honeycrisp apples baked under a buttery cinnamon oat crumble until tender.

## Ingredients
- 10 Honeycrisp or Golden Delicious apples (peel, slice)
- 3/4 cup butter (plus 2 tbsp)
- 1 3/4 cups flour
- 1 3/4 cups sugar
- 1 3/4 cups quick cooking oats
- 3 tbsp cinnamon (plus 1 1/2 tsp)
- 1/4 cup butter cut into pieces (plus 3 tbsp)

## Steps
1. Heat oven to 350˚.
2. In a bowl combine 3/4 cup plus 2 tbsp butter, flour, sugar, oats, and 2 tbsp cinnamon to form a crumble mixture. Peel and slice the apples.
3. Sprinkle the mixture over the apples in a 9x13 pan. Dot with the remaining 1/4 cup butter and sprinkle with the remaining 1 tbsp cinnamon. Bake about 1.5 hrs until apples are tender. @timer 1.5h

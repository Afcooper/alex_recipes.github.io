Overnight crescent dinner rolls started at 5PM and baked the next morning.

## Ingredients
- 3 cups real warm water
- 1/2 cup shortening
- 1 cup sugar
- 1 tsp salt
- 2 beaten eggs
- 1 tsp dry yeast
- 10 cups bread flour (10-12 c)

## Steps
1. **5PM:** Dissolve shortening in warm water. Add sugar, salt, and eggs, then mix.
2. Sprinkle dry yeast over and mix.
3. Add 2 cups flour at a time. Keep adding flour until you have a soft, non-sticky dough. Knead in the last few cups of flour.
4. Place dough in a greased bowl and cover.
5. Punch down every hour until 10 PM.
6. Roll out dough, cut into triangles with a pizza cutter, roll into crescent rolls, and place on parchment sheets.
7. Cover gently with flour sack towels and let set all night on the counter. @timer 12h
8. **8 AM:** Bake at 350 degrees. You can brush tops with cream, butter, or beaten egg.

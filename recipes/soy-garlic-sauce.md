A sticky soy-garlic glaze finished with butter and Dijon, great over salmon or chicken.

## Ingredients
- 1/4 cup low-sodium soy sauce
- 1/4 cup soy sauce
- 0.333 cup brown sugar
- 1 1/2 tbsp lemon juice
- 6 cloves garlic, medium (minced by hand)
- 2 tbsp unsalted butter
- 1 1/2 tbsp Dijon mustard

## Steps
1. **Make the glaze:** In a small saucepan over medium heat add soy sauce, brown sugar, lemon juice, and garlic. Allow to cook on medium-low until the sugar has melted, the liquid has reduced by half, and it has made a sticky glaze.
2. **Add butter:** Add half the butter and swirl until it has melted, then remove from heat. Add in the rest of the butter and swirl until it has melted.
3. **Finish:** Add Dijon mustard and stir to combine.

## Notes
Makes about 1 cup. Store in the refrigerator.

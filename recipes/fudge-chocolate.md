Classic semi-sweet chocolate fudge made in an 8x8 pan and chilled to set.

## Ingredients
- 2 1/4 cups sugar
- 0.667 cup evaporated milk (2/3 c + T)
- 1 tbsp vanilla
- 1 stick unsalted butter (8 T)
- 1 bag semi-sweet chocolate chips

## Steps
1. Line an 8x8 pan with aluminum foil and butter it.
2. Boil sugar and milk in a saucepan over medium heat for 4 minutes, stirring constantly. @timer 4m
3. Remove from heat.
4. Add chocolate chips and butter. Stir. Add vanilla.
5. Pour fudge and let cool in the refrigerator.

Creamy peanut butter fudge made with marshmallow creme, cut into squares.

## Ingredients
- 2 cups sugar
- 0.667 cup milk
- 1 cup marshmallow creme
- 1 cup peanut butter, creamy
- 1 tsp vanilla

## Steps
1. Cook sugar and milk to soft ball stage (234 degrees).
2. Add the other ingredients and mix well (Hint: add marshmallow creme before peanut butter).
3. Pour into a buttered 9 x 9 pan.
4. Cool and cut into squares.

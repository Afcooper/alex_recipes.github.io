How to freeze bread, meat, garlic, lemon juice and cookie dough for long storage.

## Steps
1. **Bread:** Wrap in plastic wrap, then wrap in aluminum foil, place in a freezer bag, and label (good for 6 months).
2. **Meat:** Wrap individually in plastic wrap, then wrap in aluminum foil, place in a freezer bag, and label.
3. **Garlic:** Microplane or finely mince garlic cloves. Measure per teaspoon. Add double the olive or avocado oil to the garlic, 1:2 (garlic to oil). Scoop into an ice cube tray.
4. **Lemons:** Squeeze juice out of lemons. Pour into ice cube trays.
5. **Cookie dough:** Form into a tube and wrap in plastic wrap, or flash freeze individually formed balls and place in a freezer bag.

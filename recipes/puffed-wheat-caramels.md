Chewy homemade caramels cooked to soft-ball stage and studded with puffed wheat.

## Ingredients
- 2 cups brown sugar
- 1 cup sugar
- 1/2 cup light corn syrup
- 1 cup cream
- 1 cup milk
- 0.333 cup butter
- 1 tsp vanilla
- 0.333 pkg puffed wheat

## Steps
1. Mix all ingredients except puffed wheat and vanilla in a sauce pan.
2. Cook slowly until it reaches soft ball stage (230-235ºF).
3. Remove from heat, add vanilla and puffed wheat. Turn into greased pan immediately. Cut when cool.

Quick-pickled red onions in a sweet-tangy cider vinegar brine.

## Ingredients
- 1 cup apple cider vinegar
- 1/4 cup water
- 1/4 cup sugar
- 1 tsp salt
- 2 medium red onions
- garlic (sliced), optional

## Steps
1. Bring water to a boil. Add water, vinegar, sugar, and salt. Stir until the sugar is dissolved. Let sit off heat for 10 minutes. @timer 10m
2. Place thinly sliced red onions in a glass mason jar. Pour the liquid mixture over, add garlic (optional).
3. Place in the refrigerator. Let marinate at least overnight. @timer 12h

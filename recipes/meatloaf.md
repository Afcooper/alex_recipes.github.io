A classic baked meatloaf with a sweet brown sugar, mustard, and ketchup topping.

## Ingredients
- 1/2 cup ketchup
- 1 egg, beaten
- 1/2 cup milk
- 1 lb ground beef or meatless crumbles
- 1/4 cup scallions
- 3/4 tsp salt
- 1/8 tsp pepper
- 1 tbsp worcestershire
- handful of oatmeal or crushed saltines
- 5 tbsp brown sugar (topping)
- 1 tsp mustard (topping)
- 0.333 cup ketchup (topping)

## Steps
1. Mix the meatloaf ingredients with your hands. Form into a loaf.
2. **Topping:** Combine the brown sugar, mustard, and ketchup and pour over the loaf.
3. Bake at 350º for 1 hour, uncovered. @timer 1h

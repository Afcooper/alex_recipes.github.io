A chilled lemon jello salad with pineapple and bananas topped with a cooked custard whipped-cream frosting.

## Ingredients
- 1 pkg lemon Jello, small package, prepared
- 1 can crushed pineapple, large (save juice)
- 12 large marshmallows, cut up (or ~2 cups minis)
- 3 bananas
- 1 cup pineapple juice
- 1 tbsp flour
- 1 egg
- 1/2 cup sugar
- 1 cup heavy whipping cream (1/2 pint)
- powdered sugar, to taste

## Steps
1. **Jello base:** Stir together the prepared lemon jello, crushed pineapple, marshmallows, and bananas; refrigerate.
2. **Cooked sauce:** In a saucepan over medium-low heat, cook the pineapple juice, flour, egg, and sugar until thick.
3. Cool the sauce in the refrigerator while making the whip topping.
4. **Whip topping:** Whip the heavy whipping cream with a mixer until peaks appear, adding powdered sugar to taste.
5. Stir the cooled sauce and whip topping together, then frost the jello.

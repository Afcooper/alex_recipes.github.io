Creamy potato salad with boiled eggs, onion, and a tangy Miracle Whip dressing.

## Ingredients
- 8 yellow gold potatoes (or 3 lbs, skin on)
- 4 boiled eggs
- 1 small-medium yellow onion
- 1 1/2 tbsp vegetable oil
- 2 cups Miracle Whip, full fat (or 15 oz)
- 0.667 tbsp yellow mustard
- 0.667 tbsp Lawry's seasoned salt
- 2 tbsp sugar

## Steps
1. Place potatoes in cold salted water. Boil on high, then simmer until tender, about 10-12 minutes. Place in cold water immediately and peel. @timer 12m
2. Boil the eggs: fill water 1 inch above eggs, place eggs in, turn off heat, cover, and let sit for 10 minutes. Place in cold water, crack all around on the side of the pan, let sit, and peel. @timer 10m
3. Cube the potatoes and slice eggs thin (or chop), then add the onion.
4. In a separate dish, mix the oil, Miracle Whip, mustard, seasoned salt, and sugar until well blended.
5. Pour the dressing over the potatoes, eggs, and onion and toss. Chill at least 3 hours. @timer 3h

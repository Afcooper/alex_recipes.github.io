Soft yeast dough rolled into ropes, tied into knots, baked, and brushed with garlic spread and parmesan.

## Ingredients
- 1 1/2 cups water (warm)
- 2 tbsp sugar
- 1 tbsp yeast
- 3 1/2 cups flour
- 1 tbsp salt

## Steps
1. Mix warm water, sugar, and yeast together. Let sit 5 min. @timer 5m
2. Add flour and salt to the water mix. Mix until smooth. Let rise for 10 min. @timer 10m
3. On a floured surface, roll dough 1/2" thick. Use a pizza cutter to cut the dough in half, then into strips (8-10").
4. Roll strips in your hands to look like ropes, then tie into knots.
5. Place knots on a greased baking sheet and let rise 10 min. @timer 10m
6. Bake at 400 degrees for 15-20 minutes. @timer 20m
7. Brush baked knots with garlic spread and sprinkle with parmesan.

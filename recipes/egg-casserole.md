Overnight strata of bread, sharp cheddar and sausage baked in an egg and milk custard.

## Ingredients
- 8 slices bread (cubed)
- 2 cups sharp cheddar cheese
- 1 lb brown & serve sausage
- 7 eggs
- 2 1/2 cups milk
- 1 can cream of mushroom soup
- 1/2 cup milk

## Steps
1. Spread bread cubes in a greased 9x13 pan. Sprinkle with cheese. Spread sausage.
2. Beat eggs. Add 2 1/2 c milk. Beat well. Pour over mixture. Refrigerate overnight. @timer 12h
3. Blend soup and 1/2 c milk. Pour over.
4. Bake at 300° for 1 1/2 hours. @timer 1.5h

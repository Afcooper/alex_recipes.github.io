A chuck roast slow-cooked over a bed of onions with chicken and beef broth.

## Ingredients
- 2 lb chuck roast
- 1 onion (white or yellow), medium
- 1/4 cup chicken broth (1/4-1/2 c)
- 1/4 cup beef broth (Better Than Bouillon roast beef) (1/4-1/2 c)

## Steps
1. Slice the onions and place on the bottom of the pot.
2. Dry the roast with paper towels. Salt and pepper.
3. Place the roast on top of the bed of onions.
4. Add the broths.
5. Set on LOW for about 5 hours. Remove excess broth if it rises more than a quarter of the way up the roast. @timer 5h

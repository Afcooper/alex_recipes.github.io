Creamy broccoli slaw with red onion, craisins and a sweet-tangy yogurt dressing.

## Ingredients
- 12 oz broccoli slaw, pkg
- 1 small red onion (chopped)
- 0.333 cup miracle whip
- 0.333 cup sugar
- 2 tbsp apple cider vinegar
- 1/2 cup plain Greek yogurt (Chobani)
- craisins (50% less sugar)

## Steps
1. Chop the red onion.
2. Mix together in a bowl the miracle whip, sugar, apple cider vinegar and yogurt.
3. Add the slaw, red onion, and craisins to the bowl and mix. Chill in the refrigerator.

## Notes
Options: Add bacon and sesame seeds.

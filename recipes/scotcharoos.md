No-bake peanut butter Rice Krispie bars topped with melted chocolate and butterscotch chips.

## Ingredients
- 1 cup white Karo syrup
- 1 cup sugar
- 1 1/2 cups peanut butter
- 5 1/2 cups Rice Krispie cereal
- 1 bag chocolate chips
- 1/2 bag butterscotch chips

## Steps
1. In a sauce pan over medium heat, melt the syrup, sugar, and peanut butter until creamy.
2. In a large mixing bowl, pour the melted mixture over the Rice Krispies. Mix.
3. Line a 9 x 13 pan with aluminum foil and spray. Pour the mixture in the pan and even it out. Place in the refrigerator.
4. In the microwave, melt the chips and pour over the krispies. Refrigerate.

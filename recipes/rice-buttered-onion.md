Minute rice cooked in buttery sauteed onion and chicken broth.

## Ingredients
- 0.333 cup onion
- 3 tbsp butter
- 1 cup Minute Rice (white)
- 12 oz chicken broth or water (12-14 oz)

## Steps
1. Finely dice onion.
2. Melt butter and onion on medium heat in a saucepan.
3. After onions are soft and translucent, remove from heat and add the rice. Pour into a bowl.
4. In the same saucepan heat the broth until boiling, add the rice mixture to the broth and boil for 5 minutes or so. Reduce heat to a simmer and cover for about 25 minutes or until broth is absorbed. @timer 25m

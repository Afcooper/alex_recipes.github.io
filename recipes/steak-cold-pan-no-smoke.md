A pan-seared steak started in a cold skillet and basted with butter and garlic to minimize smoke.

## Ingredients
- 1 steak, 1 ½" thick
- pepper and salt (salt overnight or after cooking)
- 1 tbsp butter (1-2 tbsp, optional)
- 1 clove garlic (1-2 cloves, optional)

## Steps
1. Take steak out of the refrigerator 20 minutes prior. @timer 20m
2. Pat steak dry with paper towels.
3. Pepper the steak (DO NOT salt just prior to searing).
4. Place steak in skillet, then turn heat to high.
5. Sear for 2 minutes, flip, and sear for another 2 minutes. @timer 2m
6. Reduce heat to medium (lower if you see smoke or splatter). Flip and sear for another 2 minutes. @timer 2m
7. Flip and sear for another 1 minute, or until 125˚ (medium-rare). @timer 1m
8. Add butter and mashed garlic cloves, spoon to baste, tilting the pan.
9. Temp from the side to the middle, rest 5 minutes, then salt. @timer 5m

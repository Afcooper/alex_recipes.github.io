Manicotti or cannelloni tubes stuffed with a ricotta-spinach cheese filling, baked in marinara.

## Ingredients
- 4 cups spinach (optional)
- 2 eggs
- 1 tsp garlic powder
- 1 tsp salt
- 1 tsp black pepper
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- 17 oz ricotta cheese
- 1 cup shredded cheese (mozzarella, cheddar, gruyere)
- 11 manicotti (cooked al dente) or cannelloni tubes
- marinara (store bought or homemade)

## Steps
1. Heat oven to 400 degrees.
2. **Filling:** Mix the filling ingredients in a bowl, then transfer to a gallon baggie and cut a hole in the corner.
3. Fill the tubes with the filling.
4. Pour half the marinara in the bottom of a 9x9 casserole dish.
5. Place tubes flat in the casserole. Pour remaining marinara over top and top with parmesan/mozzarella. Bake 20-25 minutes. @timer 25m

A creamy cream cheese and shrimp dip served with potato chips or crackers.

## Ingredients
- 1 tub cream cheese (large)
- 1 Hoffman House shrimp sauce
- 1 lb shrimp

## Steps
1. With a mixer, combine cream cheese and shrimp sauce.
2. Drain canned shrimp and mix into the sauce with a spoon.
3. Serve with thick potato chips or Ritz crackers.

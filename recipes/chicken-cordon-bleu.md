Baked chicken breasts layered with mushrooms, ham, and Swiss cheese under a creamy soup topping.

## Ingredients
- chicken breasts
- mushrooms
-  | slice | ham, sliced
-  | slice | Swiss cheese slices
- 1/2 can cream of chicken soup
- 1/2 can cream of mushroom soup

## Steps
1. In a baking dish, cover the chicken with mushrooms, ham, and Swiss cheese.
2. Spread the soup over the top.
3. Bake 1 hour at 350 degrees. @timer 1h

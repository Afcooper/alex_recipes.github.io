A sautéed Mexican medley of squash, zucchini, corn, peppers, and tomato.

## Ingredients
- yellow squash (parboiled), cut in small cubes
- zucchini (parboiled), cut in small cubes
- red bell pepper (roasted)
- cauliflower (parboiled)
- sweet corn (cooked)
- red onion (diced small)
- tomato (cut in small cubes)
- olive oil
- cilantro (fresh), cut up
- garlic powder
- salt and black pepper
- oregano (dried)

## Steps
1. In a large skillet add olive oil and sauté onion for 2 minutes. @timer 2m
2. Then add the rest of the ingredients.

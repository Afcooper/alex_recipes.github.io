A cooked butterscotch-chocolate frosting thickened with egg yolk for German chocolate cake.

## Ingredients
- 1 cup brown sugar
- 1 cup evaporated milk
- 0.667 cup butterscotch & chocolate chips
- 1/2 cup water
- 1 tbsp Karo syrup
- 1 egg yolk, beaten
- 2 tbsp butter

## Steps
1. Combine in a saucepan: brown sugar, evaporated milk, butterscotch & chocolate chips, water, and Karo syrup.
2. Add 1 beaten egg yolk.
3. Cook over medium heat, stirring until thick.
4. Remove from heat and add 2 tbsp butter.

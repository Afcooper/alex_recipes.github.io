Creamy mayo-based hibachi-style sauce with ketchup, sweet chili and sriracha.

## Ingredients
- 0.667 cup mayonnaise
- 2 tbsp ketchup
- 3 tbsp sweet chili sauce
- 1 tsp Worcestershire sauce
- 1/2 tsp onion powder
- 1/2 tsp garlic powder
- 1/2 tsp paprika
- 3/4 tsp sriracha
- salt and pepper to taste

## Steps
1. Combine all ingredients in a bowl and stir to combine.
2. Keep refrigerated in a sealed jar until ready to use.

## Notes
Serve with onion rings, sushi, sweet potato fries, shrimp, chicken, hamburgers, etc.

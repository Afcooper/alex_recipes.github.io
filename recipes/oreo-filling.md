Fluffy marshmallow-and-shortening filling that mimics the creme center of an Oreo.

## Ingredients
- 2 jars marshmallow cream (7 oz jars)
- 1/2 cup shortening
- 0.667 cup powdered sugar
- 1 tsp vanilla extract (1–1.5 tsp)
- 4 tsp very hot water (makes easier to spread)
- 1/4 tsp salt

## Steps
1. Combine marshmallow cream, shortening, powdered sugar, and vanilla in a bowl and mix well with an electric mixer on high until fluffy.
2. In a separate small bowl, combine the salt with the hot water and stir until dissolved. Add to the fluff mixture.
3. Mix on high until combined.

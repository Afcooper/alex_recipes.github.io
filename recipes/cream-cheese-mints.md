Soft cream cheese mints flavored with almond and shaped in molds or cutters.

## Ingredients
- 4 oz cream cheese (room temp)
- 1 tbsp butter
- 4 cups powdered sugar
- 1/2 tsp almond extract
- 1/8 tsp vanilla
- granulated sugar
- food coloring

## Steps
1. Place cream cheese in a mixing bowl. Using an electric mixer, start adding powdered sugar until you reach the consistency of play dough (use just less than a 2 lb bag of powdered sugar). Add more powdered sugar if too sticky.
2. Add a drop or two of flavoring and mix to incorporate. Add food coloring and knead into the dough.
3. Roll dough in granulated sugar and press into a mint mold or cut with mini cookie cutters. Store in the fridge.

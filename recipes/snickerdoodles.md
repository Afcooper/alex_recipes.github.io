Soft cinnamon-sugar cookies leavened with cream of tartar and baking soda.

## Ingredients
- 1 cup butter (or 1/2 c butter + 1/4 c LOL butter spread with canola or olive oil + 1/4 c shortening)
- 1 1/2 cups sugar
- 2 eggs
- 1 tsp vanilla
- 1/2 tsp salt
- 2 3/4 cups flour
- 1 tsp baking soda
- 2 tsp cream of tartar

## Steps
1. Mix the first 3 ingredients (butter, sugar, eggs), then sift and stir in the rest.
2. Roll into balls and roll in a cinnamon-sugar mixture. Place on a cookie sheet. Place a divot in the middle if you want flatter cookies.
3. Bake at 350 degrees for 8-10 minutes. Makes 2 dozen. @timer 10m

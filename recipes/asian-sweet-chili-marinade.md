A whisked-together sweet chili, honey, and lime marinade that doubles as a finishing glaze.

## Ingredients
- 1/4 cup Asian sweet chili sauce (Mae Ploy)
- 1/4 cup honey
- 1/4 cup lime juice (or less)
- 3 tbsp reduced sodium soy sauce
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- 3 cloves garlic cloves or garlic powder

## Steps
1. Whisk all ingredients together.
2. Use half to marinate (shrimp, etc.); discard that marinade after using on meat. Use the remaining half as a glaze when finished cooking.

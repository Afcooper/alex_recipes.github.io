Medium shell pasta in a creamy, spicy tomato sauce with parmesan and mozzarella.

## Ingredients
- 4 tbsp butter
- 1/2 onion (chopped)
- 3 cloves garlic (minced)
- 3 tbsp tomato paste
- 1 1/4 cups heavy cream
- 0.333 cup pasta water
- 1/2 cup parmesan (shredded)
- 1/2 cup mozzarella (shredded)
- shell pasta (medium)
- 1 tsp paprika
- 1 tsp black pepper
- 1 tsp red chili flakes
- 1 tsp chili powder

## Steps
1. Melt butter. Add onions and garlic and sauté until they shrink.
2. Add tomato paste, then add heavy cream and seasonings. Mix until sauce forms.
3. Add pasta water and cheese. Mix. Then mix in the pasta with more cheese.

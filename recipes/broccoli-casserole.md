Cheesy baked broccoli and rice casserole with cream of mushroom soup and Cheese Whiz.

## Ingredients
- 1 stick butter (1/2 c)
- 2 boxes chopped frozen broccoli
- 1/4 cup chopped onion (white)
- 2 1/4 cups Minute rice
- 2 1/4 cups water
- 2 cans cream of mushroom soup
- 1 jar Cheese Whiz (16 oz)

## Steps
1. Melt butter in a sauce pan over medium-low heat, add broccoli and onion.
2. Cook rice and water.
3. In a 9x13 casserole dish add rice, soup and Cheese Whiz. Mix in broccoli.
4. Bake for 20-25 minutes at 350°. @timer 25m

## Notes
Option: add a can of mushrooms.

Cubed red potatoes baked in garlic butter until tender.

## Ingredients
- 5 lb red potatoes
- 3 sticks butter
- garlic powder (1 tbsp+)

## Steps
1. Melt butter, add garlic.
2. Wash potatoes, cut into cubes.
3. Place cubes in a 9x13" pan. Pour melted butter and garlic over potatoes. Stir so all are covered.
4. Bake at 350ºF until tender.

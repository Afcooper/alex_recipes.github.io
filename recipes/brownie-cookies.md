Fudgy cookies made from boxed brownie mix with espresso and semi-sweet chocolate chips.

## Ingredients
- 20 oz brownie mix (Duncan Hines)
- 2 eggs
- 1/2 cup butter, unsalted, room temp
- 2 tbsp brewed cooled espresso
- salt (pinch)
- 1 1/4 cups semi-sweet chocolate chips

## Steps
1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees.
2. Mix all ingredients together.
3. Refrigerate for a minimum of 1/2 hour. @timer 30m
4. Line a cookie sheet with parchment paper.
5. Bake ~12 minutes. @timer 12m

## Notes
Options: add a pinch of salt; oil the parchment with bacon drippings.

Buttery, Worcestershire-and-garlic seasoned mix of Chex cereals, pretzels and peanuts made in the microwave.

## Ingredients
- 7 tbsp butter
- 2 1/4 tbsp Worcestershire sauce
- 1 tbsp garlic powder
- 3/4 tsp sugar
- 1/2 tsp onion powder
- 1 tsp salt
- 2 1/2 cups Rice Chex cereal
- 2 1/2 cups Corn Chex cereal
- 2 cups Wheat Chex cereal
- 1 cup stick pretzels
- 1 cup peanuts

## Steps
1. Melt butter in the microwave in a 9 x 13 glass dish. Stir in Worcestershire, garlic powder, onion powder, salt, and sugar. Mix well.
2. Add cereals, peanuts, and pretzels. Mix well to coat all ingredients.
3. Microwave a total of 5-6 minutes, 2 minutes at a time, stirring in between. Pour onto paper towels to dry. @timer 5m

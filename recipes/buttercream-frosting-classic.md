Classic American buttercream beaten smooth with vanilla and cream for piping or spreading.

## Ingredients
- 7 cups powdered sugar
- 2 cups unsalted butter (room temp)
- 1 tbsp vanilla
- 1/2 tsp salt
- 3 tbsp heavy cream or half n half

## Steps
1. Beat the butter on medium speed for 30 seconds until smooth.
2. Mix in vanilla extract and salt.
3. Slowly add powdered sugar, alternating with splashes of cream.
4. Mix until fully incorporated.
5. If frosting is too thick add additional cream (1 tbsp at a time). If frosting is too thin add more powdered sugar.

## Notes
Store frosting in the fridge for a month, freezer up to 3 months.

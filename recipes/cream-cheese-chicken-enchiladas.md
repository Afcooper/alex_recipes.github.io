Flour tortillas filled with creamy chicken, rolled with red enchilada sauce and cheese, and baked.

## Ingredients
- 1 tbsp olive oil
- 1/2 cup chopped yellow or white onion
- 3 cloves fresh garlic, chopped
- 1/2 fresh jalapeno or fresno chile, chopped (optional)
- 5 chicken thighs (about 5)
- 8 oz cream cheese, softened at room temp
- 4 oz sour cream
- 1/2 tsp kosher salt
- 10 flour tortillas, soft taco size (10-12)
- 2 cans red enchilada sauce (10 oz each)
- 8 oz block cheese, shredded, cheddar (about 3 cups total)
- 1 can refried beans

## Steps
1. **Cook aromatics and chicken:** Heat olive oil and saute onion, garlic, and jalapeno (if using). Cook the chicken thighs through, then shred or chop.
2. **Make filling:** Mix the chicken with softened cream cheese, sour cream, and kosher salt.
3. **Assemble:** Spread refried beans and filling onto flour tortillas, roll up, and place seam-side down in a baking dish over a layer of red enchilada sauce. Top with remaining enchilada sauce and shredded cheddar cheese.
4. **Bake:** Bake until heated through and the cheese is melted and bubbly.

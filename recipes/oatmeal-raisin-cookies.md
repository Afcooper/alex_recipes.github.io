Soft oatmeal cookies loaded with plumped raisins and Quaker quick oats.

## Ingredients
- 1 1/2 cups raisins (place in boiling water 10 min, then dry)
- 1 1/2 cups flour
- 1/2 tsp salt
- 1/2 tsp baking powder
- 1 tsp cinnamon powder
- 8 oz butter (room temp ~30 min)
- 1 cup brown sugar (packed)
- 1 cup sugar
- 3 eggs (large)
- 3 cups Quaker Quick 1 min Oats

## Steps
1. Preheat oven to 350 on convection bake.
2. Sift flour, salt and baking powder into a bowl.
3. Cream butter and sugar, then add eggs.
4. Mix in the flour mixture, then stir in oats and raisins.
5. Roll and flatten the dough. Bake 11 minutes, rotate, and bake 3-4 minutes more. @timer 11m

## Notes
Makes 3 trays of 16.

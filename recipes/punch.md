A fruity party punch with raspberry Kool-Aid, lemonade, soda, and frozen strawberries.

## Ingredients
- 1 qt water
- 1 pkg raspberry Kool-Aid
- 6 oz frozen lemonade
- 1 cup sugar
- 16 oz 7-Up
- 16 oz Squirt
- 1 pt frozen strawberries

## Steps
1. Mix water, Kool-Aid, lemonade, and sugar.
2. Add 7-Up, Squirt, and strawberries at serving time.

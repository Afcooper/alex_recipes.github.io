Soft peanut butter cookies sweetened with dark brown sugar and a touch of honey.

## Ingredients
- 1.667 cups all-purpose flour
- 1 tsp baking soda
- 1 tsp salt
- 1 1/2 cups dark brown sugar
- 1 cup creamy peanut butter
- 2 tbsp honey
- 1 tsp vanilla extract
- 4 tbsp melted butter
- 2 eggs

## Steps
1. **Mix:** Mix dry ingredients and set aside. Mix wet ingredients. Add dry ingredients to wet.
2. **Shape:** Line a cookie sheet with parchment paper. Scoop into balls and flatten to ~2 inch diameter.
3. **Bake:** Bake at 350 degrees for 10 minutes. @timer 10m

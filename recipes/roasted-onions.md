Sweet onions roasted with butter and balsamic until golden and tender.

## Ingredients
- 2 sweet onions, large (yellow or vidalia)
- 2 tbsp butter, melted
- 1 tsp balsamic vinegar
- 1/8 tsp salt, kosher
- 1/8 tsp pepper, fresh ground

## Steps
1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees.
2. Whisk melted butter and balsamic vinegar.
3. Cut onion into 1/2 inch slices.
4. Place onions in a greased 9x13 baking dish (glass or ceramic). Brush with butter mixture.
5. Pour water into the casserole dish. Season with salt and pepper.
6. Roast 40-50 minutes until golden and tender. @timer 40m

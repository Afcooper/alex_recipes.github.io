A glossy chocolate topping cooked on the stovetop and poured over brownies.

## Ingredients
- 1 1/2 cups sugar
- 6 tbsp milk
- 6 tbsp butter
- 1 bag chocolate chips

## Steps
1. Mix sugar, milk, and butter in a saucepan over medium heat until it boils.
2. Remove from heat.
3. Add the bag of chocolate chips.
4. Pour over brownies.

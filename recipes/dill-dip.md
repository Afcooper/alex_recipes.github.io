Creamy sour cream and mayo dip seasoned with dill, celery salt, and onion.

## Ingredients
- 1/2 cup sour cream (full fat)
- 1/4 cup mayo (full fat, includes sugar)
- dry dill weed
- celery salt
- dried chopped onion
- garlic powder (less than other seasonings)

## Steps
1. Mix all in a bowl. Let sit overnight or several hours to marinate and thicken. @timer 12h

Chicken gently simmered until tender, shredded, and finished with a reduced broth.

## Ingredients
- chicken
- water (enough to cover chicken)

## Steps
1. **Simmer:** In a heavy-bottomed saucepan combine all ingredients. Add just enough water to cover the chicken. Turn heat to high and bring to a boil. Remove any scum or foam that floats to the top. Adjust heat to a gentle simmer and cook for 30 minutes or until chicken is very tender. @timer 30m
2. **Shred:** Transfer to a cutting board and use 2 forks to shred the chicken.
3. **Reduce broth:** After removing chicken, boil the liquid until it has reduced to one third. Spoon about 1 cup over the shredded chicken. Eat within 2-3 days.

## Notes
Chicken & broth will keep in the fridge up to 3 days or frozen for 1 month.

Can be cooked in slow cooker on LOW for 4-6 hours.

Coffee-and-Nesquik buttercream frosting flavored with brewed coffee and vanilla.

## Ingredients
- 0.333 cup Nesquik mix
- 3 cups powdered sugar
- 0.333 cup strong hot brewed coffee (cooled in fridge)
- 0.333 cup softened butter
- 1/8 tsp salt
- 1 tsp vanilla

## Steps
1. Cool the brewed coffee in the fridge before using.
2. Beat softened butter with powdered sugar, Nesquik mix, salt and vanilla, then add the cooled coffee and mix until smooth and spreadable.

Boiled rotini tossed with fresh veggies and a sweet-tangy Italian oil and vinegar dressing.

## Ingredients
- 8 oz rotini noodles, boiled (1 box)
- cut-up veggies: carrots, tomatoes, cauliflower
- cheese, cubed (optional)
- 1/2 cup vegetable oil (dressing)
- 1/2 cup apple cider vinegar (dressing)
- 1/2 cup sugar (dressing)
- 1 pkg Good Seasons Italian dressing, dry (dressing)

## Steps
1. **Dressing:** Whisk together vegetable oil, apple cider vinegar, sugar and dry Good Seasons Italian dressing.
2. Pour dressing over noodles and veggies and mix.

Rich, lightly sweet frosting made from mascarpone, cream cheese and sifted powdered sugar.

## Ingredients
- 4 oz mascarpone cheese (room temp)
- 4 oz cream cheese (room temp)
- 3 cups powdered sugar (sifted)

## Steps
1. Beat the room-temperature mascarpone and cream cheese together until smooth, then gradually add the sifted powdered sugar and beat until fluffy.

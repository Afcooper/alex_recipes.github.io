Lighter broccoli casserole with sauteed onions and mushrooms, brown rice and quinoa, and sharp cheddar.

## Ingredients
- 1 box chopped frozen chopped broccoli
- 1 small onion, chopped (yellow or white)
- 1 bag Seeds of Change brown rice & quinoa (90 sec)
- 0.667 can cream of mushroom soup
- 1 cup shredded sharp cheddar cheese
- mushrooms (crimini)
- butter and oil (for sauteing)

## Steps
1. Saute onions in butter and oil until transparent and browning, add broccoli and mushrooms.
2. Stir together skillet items with soup and cheese and the prepared Seeds of Change grains in a casserole dish.
3. Bake for 20-25 minutes at 350°. @timer 25m

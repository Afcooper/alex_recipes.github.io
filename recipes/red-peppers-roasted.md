Broiler-roasted red peppers charred, peeled, and ready to use or freeze.

## Ingredients
- red peppers

## Steps
1. Line rimmed baking sheet with foil.
2. Heat oven to broil or 500˚ (place rack 4" from top).
3. Place baking sheet in oven.
4. Wash/dry and cut peppers in half, remove seeds/ribs.
5. When oven is done preheating, remove baking sheet.
6. Place peppers skin side up, lay flat, and place in oven.
7. Roast (5-10 min) until black/charred. @timer 10m
8. Flip and roast another 5 min. @timer 5m
9. Remove, place in a bowl, cover with plastic wrap.
10. Let cool, remove skin.
11. To freeze: place 2 pieces in freezer zip lock bags.

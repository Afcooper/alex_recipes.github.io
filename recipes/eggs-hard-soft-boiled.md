Method for boiling eggs to soft or hard doneness, then peeling cleanly under water.

## Ingredients
- eggs
- water

## Steps
1. Fill a saucepan with water 1" higher than the eggs.
2. Bring to a boil.
3. Add eggs.
4. Reduce heat to a gentle boil. Cook 4-5 minutes for soft-boiled or 8-10 minutes for hard-boiled. @timer 8m
5. Empty the boiling water and run eggs under cold water.
6. Crack eggs gently on the ends and roll lengthwise on the side of the pan to crack.
7. Peel under water.

## Notes
4-5 minutes = soft-boiled egg.

8-10 minutes = hard-boiled egg.

A quick skillet of ground beef simmered with chicken gumbo soup, brown sugar, ketchup, and oatmeal.

## Ingredients
- 1 can Chicken Gumbo soup
- 1 lb ground beef
- 3 tbsp brown sugar
- 3 tbsp ketchup
- 2 tsp mustard
- handful oatmeal (instant or minute)

## Steps
1. In a skillet, brown the beef and drain.
2. Add the remaining ingredients over medium-low heat, until the oatmeal is soft.

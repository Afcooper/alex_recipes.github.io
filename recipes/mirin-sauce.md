A quick sweet-savory mirin and soy sauce thickened with cornstarch for meat, veg, rice, or sushi.

## Ingredients
- 1 clove garlic, minced/grated
- 1 tsp cornstarch
- 1 tbsp sugar
- 2 tbsp mirin
- 2 tbsp soy sauce

## Steps
1. **Combine:** Place all ingredients in a bowl and whisk until well combined.
2. **Thicken:** Transfer to a saucepan on medium heat. Heat until bubbles, reduce to low, stir frequently (watch closely) until thickened (consistency of oil), about 3-5 minutes. @timer 3m

## Notes
Double recipe to yield 1/3-1/2 cup.

Add to meat, veg, rice, sushi.

Yellow cake baked over spiraled peaches and dark brown sugar for a caramelized upside-down finish.

## Ingredients
- 1 can sliced peaches (drained), Bettergoods amaretto
- 1 box yellow cake mix, Pillsbury (prepare)
- 3/4 stick butter
- dark brown sugar, to cover peaches and bottom

## Steps
1. **Prep pans:** Use two 8" round cake pans. Cut parchment to fit the bottom, spray the bottom with oil, place parchment on the bottom, then grease with butter on the bottom and sides.
2. Place peaches on the bottom in a spiral pattern.
3. Cover all with brown sugar.
4. Pour prepared cake batter over the top, divided evenly.
5. Bake per cake instructions.

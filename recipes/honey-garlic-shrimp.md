Shrimp marinated in a honey, soy, and garlic sauce, then quickly seared in a skillet.

## Ingredients
- 0.333 cup honey
- 1/4 cup soy sauce
- 2 cloves garlic
- mirin (splash, optional)
- 1 lb medium uncooked shrimp, peeled & deveined
- 2 tsp oil
- green onion (for garnish)

## Steps
1. **Make marinade:** Whisk honey, soy sauce, mirin, and garlic in a bowl. Half the marinade is used to marinate the shrimp in the fridge, half for serving with the shrimp.
2. **Marinate:** Place shrimp in a sealable container with 1/2 the marinade, shake, and allow to marinate in refrigerator for 15 min or up to 8-12 hrs. Cover and refrigerate remaining marinade. @timer 15m
3. **Cook:** Heat oil in skillet over medium-high heat, place shrimp in skillet (discard used marinade). Cook shrimp on one side for 45 sec, flip shrimp over and pour in remaining marinade for about another 2 minutes.

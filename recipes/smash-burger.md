Thin, crispy-edged smash burgers cooked on a hot cast iron or stainless skillet.

## Ingredients
- 1 lb ground beef 80/20 (1-1.5 lbs)

## Steps
1. **Form balls:** Make 6 balls with 1 lb 80/20 ground beef. Refrigerate; keep cold before moving to skillet. Cut squares of parchment to place on top of the balls when heating.
2. **Smash:** Heat skillet to medium-high. Place a ball on the skillet, set parchment on top, and smash to desired thickness with a spatula (smash the sides more than the middle).
3. **Cook:** Cook 2 minutes. Turn, salt and pepper, and cook 1 minute more. Remove from skillet. @timer 2m

## Notes
Use a cast iron skillet or stainless steel frying pan.

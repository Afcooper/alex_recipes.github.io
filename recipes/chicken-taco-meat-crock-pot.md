Shredded chicken thighs slow-cooked with taco seasoning, salsa and broth.

## Ingredients
- 4 chicken thighs (4-5)
- 1 pkt taco seasoning
- salsa
- chicken broth

## Steps
1. Empty the taco seasoning into a zip lock bag with the chicken thighs. Pour about ½ can of chicken broth in crock pot. Place a heaping large spoonful of salsa on top of each thigh. Set on low for 4 hours. Shred when done. @timer 4h
